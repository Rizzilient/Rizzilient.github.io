Some Background Information:

The Stock list Key above analizes an API that is composed of any stock possible. These stocks are traded in all markets present in the world, ranging from the famous New York Stock Exchange, all the way to the Australian Stock Exchange. Just enter a stock symbol, and find out more information!

Currencies are the different ways that nations of the world describe wealth. Most countries have their own currency, and this directly translates to their stock markets, as well as cryptocurrencies, ETF's and much more. Each currency has a value meaning that one nations currency may be worth pennies in another nation of the world.
